# Diggr World

Version: MVP v1

---

# Philosophy

Diggr is not a marketplace.

It is a world built around discovering record stores.

Users do not search for inventory.

They search for places.

Every store has its own personality.

Every discovery has its own story.

Treasure hunting is the product.

Finding a record should feel like discovering a hidden café while travelling.

---

The world consists of six layers.

# Stores

Stores are the heart of Diggr.

Inventory changes every week.

Store personality changes slowly.

Users should choose stores because of personality,

not because of inventory.

Each store has:

• DNA

• Match Score

• Description

• Recent Finds

• Community Recommendations

# Communities

Communities are the soul of Diggr.

People follow taste, not popularity.

Every community connects collectors, stores and discoveries.

Unlike traditional forums, communities are lightweight editorial spaces rather than discussion boards.

# World Rules

Every discovery starts with a store.

Albums never exist independently.

Collectors follow taste before popularity.

Stores are remembered by personality, not inventory.

Treasure Logs tell stories, not reviews.

Communities are editorial spaces rather than social feeds.

Diggr should always feel calm, curious and rewarding.

Every interaction should encourage exploration.

The world should grow through discoveries rather than transactions.
