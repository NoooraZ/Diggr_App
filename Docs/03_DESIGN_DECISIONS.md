# 03_DESIGN_DECISIONS

# Why Treasure Map?

Google Maps helps users navigate.

Diggr helps users discover.

Therefore the map should feel exploratory rather than geographically accurate.

---

# Why Store DNA?

Inventory changes every day.

Store personality changes slowly.

Users choose stores based on personality.

Not inventory.

---

# Why Recent Finds?

Inventory cannot realistically stay synchronized.

Recent Finds provide a lightweight, community-driven approximation of store inventory.

---

# Why People Like You?

People discover niche stores through people with similar taste.

Not through ratings.

Recommendation is taste-based rather than popularity-based.
