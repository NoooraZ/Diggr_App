# Diggr

> A taste-driven vinyl discovery app for collectors, treasure hunters and curious beginners.

Diggr helps people discover record stores that match their music taste.

Instead of searching for albums, users discover places.

Every store has its own DNA.

Every discovery tells a story.

---

## Philosophy

Diggr is not an e-commerce platform.

It celebrates the joy of crate digging.

Inventory changes.

Store personality lasts.

The experience is inspired by:

- Letterboxd
- Discogs
- Monocle Travel Guides
- Independent Record Shops

---
